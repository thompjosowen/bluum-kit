Empowering Urban
Gardeners

Bluum-Kit is an innovative urban agriculture company on a mission to
make urban gardening accessible and sustainable. We provide modular
planter-box systems that can be 3D printed by anyone, anywhere, along
with free 3D print fils, educational resources, and a supportive community
for urban gardeners.

Making Urban Gardening Accessible and Sustainable

At Bluum-Kit, we are on a mission to make urban gardening accessible and
sustainable for everyone. Through our innovative modular-planter box
systems and free 3D print files, we empower individuals to grow their own
green sprouts, no matter where they live.
